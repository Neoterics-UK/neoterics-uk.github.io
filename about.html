To be a Neoteric by google definition, means that you're a modern person; Someone who is accepting of & advocates for new ideas! However, here we take it one step further. We don't just believe in 'new' ideas, we believe in new, FAIR ideas. To be a Neoteric to us, means to be someone who wants to change the country & the world for the better. No matter your race, religion, age, gender, occupation, sexuality or background, you're welcome here just how you should be welcome everywhere! This being our goal, to change this country to a place where movements like this no longer have to exist, because opportunity will be equal and discrimination won't exist!
